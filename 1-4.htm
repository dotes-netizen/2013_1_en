Hsing-Wu Huang was a supervisor in Formosa Plastic Group, and now is: [Note 1] [Note 2]

  • Talent conducting instructor of all libraries in Chiayi County
  • Conducting instructor of Study Association for elementary school and junior middle school teachers in Changhua County
  • Conducting instructor of shadow play in An-Jing Elementary School
  • Executive Manager of Overall Developing Folklore Class for Meishan Township
  • Jingxin Folk Arts Promotion Association
  • Guiding instructor of Teacher Advanced Study in Changhua County, Taichung County, and Chiayi County
  • Conducting instructor of An-Jing Elementary School Clubs
  • Conducting instructor of Folk Arts Group and Teacher of Resource Class in Hsien-Hsi Junior High School and He Mei Junior High School
  • Conducting instructor of Xinhua Vocational High School Clubs.
  • Executive Manager of Overall Developing Folklore Class for Meishan Township in Council of Cultural Affairs project.
  • Folk arts instructor of Township Libraries for Changhua County (Xianxi, Xihu), Yunlin County (Kouhu, Shuilin), Chiayi County (Meishan, Dalin, Minhsiung, Tungshi, Budai, Potzi), Tainan County (Shanshang, Madou, Guantian, Liujia, Hsinshi, Yungkang, Yanshui, and Nansi). The major folk arts he teaches are dough, shadow play, and lantern.

Used to serve in: [Note 1] [Note 2]

  • National shadow play competition and performance judge for Bureau of Culture in Kaohsiung County (1999-2011)
