Plan > Vision

When Teacher Huang was young, he set up the goal to become a teacher because he knew the best time in his life would be when he passes on his knowledge and sees his students’ smiling faces.

Nowadays, Teacher Huang is even more confident in what he does, whether it is the work, career, or even his own education and community work. He continues the work of the traditional art that he learned from his brother Hsin-pin, Master Fu-neng Hsu, and even the elders of the streets; moreover, he studies the related history. Still, Teacher Huang continues his childhood vision and passes on his knowledge to the children of younger generations. In spite of the low salary and various frustrations, he calmly faces all problems. As he sees the focused look on his students, he thinks it is worth his efforts.
↑Teacher Huang is attentively teaching students about the making of shadow puppetry.


In the future, he mostly wants to engage in promoting education. He always wants to make his hometown a learning place for traditional art, environmental consciousness, and more. He also looks for various traditional arts to learn, including sugar painting, sugar blowing, and more. How can he promote sugar blowing when one can only learn it for self-entertainment? We know he will do his best to guide the next generation to know all these arts.
↑ The students were amazed when watching the teacher vividly perform shadow puppetry.

These are things he has experienced from the society since his youth. He wants to promote this place, his hometown – Hsienhsi.

(The text and pictures on these pages were based on interview transcriptions, photographs and writing taken by the project team)
Hsien-Hsi Junior High School, Changhua County, Taiwan
